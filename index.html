This is Geom Hacks

We strive to bring together students during these times and help them create through our 48 hour virtual hackathon. Our hackathon will take place June 13-14 2020

Virtual

Our hackathon is completely virtual. It will mostly take place over Discord and Zoom.

Projects

Projects will be submitted over github and prizes will be given out to the best of these.

Who can attend

Anyone in highschool or middle school can "attend". You would also need to have internet access and a computer at home.

Answers to FAQ's

The Hackathon is free.
You would need a Discord, Zoom and Github account.
Prizes will be either mailed or virtual
